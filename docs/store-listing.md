## Full description (max 4000 characters)
```
Book, train, and manage your fitness journey — all in one app.

PSAS (Pegasus Sports & Psychology Performance) puts class bookings, personal training, and membership management in your pocket. Whether you are joining a group class, booking one-on-one coaching, or reserving gym facilities, PSAS keeps your schedule organised and your credits up to date.

WHAT YOU CAN DO
• Browse the timetable and book group fitness classes in a few taps
• Book one-on-one personal training appointments with your preferred coach
• Reserve gym facilities and equipment access
• Join the waiting list for full classes — get booked automatically the moment a spot opens up
• Track your membership plan and class credits at a glance
• Cancel a booking anytime and get your credit refunded automatically
• Get instant email confirmations with a calendar invite for every booking
• Pay securely for memberships via card (Stripe) or QR code
• Apply coupon codes at checkout
• Keep your profile up to date so we can reach you about your bookings

BUILT FOR YOUR TRAINERS AND STUDIO TOO
Trainers can manage their schedule, submit requests, and track session history. Studio admins can manage members, review and resolve requests, track transactions, and keep an eye on daily activity — all from the same app.

Download PSAS and take the hassle out of booking your next session.
```
